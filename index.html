Is it time to upgrade your insulation?

There are ways you can tell if your insulation is damaged or not functioning properly. In addition to rising energy costs, you may need new insulation if you're dealing with:

Odd or cold drafts throughout your property

Frequent leaks or moisture issues

Rats, rodents, insects or other pests
